Your resume is only one piece of the puzzle. Over 90% of recruiters check GitHub or LinkedIn before making a hiring decision. HireLens analyzes both and tells you exactly what to fix.

Why profile analysis matters

1
First impressions happen before the interview
Recruiters spend an average of 6 seconds on a LinkedIn profile. A weak headline or empty summary means you never get the callback — no matter how good your resume is.
2
GitHub is your live portfolio
For developers, GitHub shows real work — not just what you claim. Repos with no README, stale commits, or no pinned projects signal low effort to hiring managers.
3
Keywords decide if you're even found
LinkedIn's search algorithm ranks profiles by keyword match. Missing industry terms in your headline means recruiters searching for your exact skills simply won't find you.

What you get with each analysis

Profile score
Overall strength rating with per-section breakdown so you know where you stand.
Prioritized fixes
Critical issues listed first so you fix what actually moves the needle.
Keyword gaps
Skills and terms missing from your profile compared to your target job market.
Red flag detection
Things that actively hurt your chances — identified and explained clearly.
